Camel.Vec4

Camel.Vec4 is the math about the vector has four elements inside it.

Members

vec

Hold the Float32Array(4).

How to use:

var vec1 = new Camel.Vec4(1.0, 2.0, 5.0, 0.0);
console.log(vec1.vec);

Constructor

Camel.Vec4(x, y, z, w)

The constructor of Camel.Vec4 has four params:

  • x:number - x-coord of the vector.
  • y:number - y-coord of the vector.
  • z:number - z-coord of the vector.
  • w:number - w-coord of the vector.

How to use:

var vec1 = new Camel.Vec4(1.1, 2.1, 4.1, 0.0);
console.log(vec1);
