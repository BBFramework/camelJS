Camel.Light

Camel.Light class collects methods and members about basic lighting.

Extended by Camel.Vec3, Camel.Color.

Constructor

Camel.Light(x, y, z)

The constructor of Camel.Light has three params:

  • x:number - x-coord of the position.
  • y:number - y-coord of the position.
  • z:number - z-coord of the position.

How to use:

var light = new Camel.Light(1.1, 2.1, 4.1);
console.log(light);
