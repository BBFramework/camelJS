Camel.Perspective

Camel.Perspective is the 4x4 matrix math for 3D perspective view.

Members

mat

Hold the Float32Array(16).

How to use:

var perspective = new Camel.Perspective(45.0, 2.1, 1.0, 1000.0);
console.log(perspective.mat);

Constructor

Camel.Perspective(angle, aspect, near, far)

The constructor of Camel.Perspective has four params:

  • angle:number - The field of view.
  • aspect:number - The aspest ratio.
  • near:number - The nearest of view.
  • far:number - The farest of view.

How to use:

var perspective = new Camel.Perspective(45.0, 2.1, 1.0, 1000.0);
console.log(perspective);
